What are the Meteoriks?

The Meteoriks are an award to honor the best productions that the demoscene has to offer, and are presented annually at the Revision demoparty over the Easter weekend.

Following a surge of activity in the Executable Graphics field, the Meteoriks introduced a category for Best Executable Graphics in 2021. You can see the nominees and winner below.
